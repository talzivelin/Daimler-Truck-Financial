› Home / Why Daimler Truck Financial

The best vehicles.
The best financing.

No lender understands your business better than Daimler Truck Financial. As the captive commercial finance provider for Daimler Trucks North America, our priority is financing commercial vehicles, including Freightliner Trucks, Western Star trucks, SelecTrucks, Sprinter vans, Thomas Built Buses and Fuso. And we've been doing so for almost 40 years, creating specialized financing and lease solutions tailored to our customers' business.

Our unequaled experience in the trucking industry, combined with our position as an exclusive commercial finance provider, make Daimler Truck Financial the smart choice for anyone looking to run their business more efficiently and profitably. In fact, between our operations center in Fort Worth, Texas, and our corporate headquarters in Farmington Hills, Michigan, we have over 300 commercial finance specialists managing a portfolio of more than $7 billion.

So whether you want to build commercial credit, preserve your liquid resources for operating needs or obtain a truly customized solution for your business, Daimler Truck Financial is the partner who will be with you throughout the lifetime of your vehicle and beyond.

“I was able to help two customers: one who wanted to optimize his fleet's capacity and a new customer who wanted to expand his business.”
